Test Your Knowledge - Storage Types

How To

To test your knowledge, simply drag and drop the orange rounded rectangles onto the grey rounded rectangles with the correct categroy of storage type. Note that one medium of storage can be in two different sections. A there will be a total of ten objects from all of the sections. Once you think you have categorised all of the objects, click the Check Your Answers button to get a percentage mark.
You can remove objects from sections by clicking the delete button next to the object in the section.
You can also restart by clicking the Reset button.
